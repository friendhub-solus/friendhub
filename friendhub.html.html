How it works

Three steps between you and a new conversation.

01 / Enter the lobby

Drop in a few interests

Type a word or two — "movies," "night owl," "startups" — or skip it and go in completely blind.

02 / We find a match

The radar finds someone

We pair you with another person online right now. No swiping, no waiting for approval — it just happens.

03 / Start talking

Say hi, see where it goes

Chat as long as you want. Not feeling it? Tap "New stranger" and the radar starts sweeping again.

Anonymous doesn't mean unsafe.

FriendHub is built for spontaneous, low-stakes conversation — not for anything else. A few ground rules keep it that way.

  • 18+
    Adults onlyFriendHub is restricted to users 18 and older. No exceptions.
  • ⛔
    One tap to leaveDisconnect from any conversation instantly, no explanation needed.
  • ▲
    Report & blockEvery chat has a report button. Reported accounts are reviewed and can be banned.
  • ✎
    Nothing is savedConversations aren't stored once a chat ends — there's no history to leak.
